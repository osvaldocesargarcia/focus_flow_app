
## 🎯The goal is to develop the FocusFlow App. Following the requirements of the Frontend Developer challenge, you are to develop a task management application designed for high productivity.

  
Below are the requirements and technical details. We have chosen a format that gives you the freedom to make the architectural decisions you deem most appropriate, showcasing your vision of a modern and scalable frontend.

### 📋 Technical Test: Frontend Developer (Angular + Tailwind)

Goal: Develop a task list application (FocusFlow App) that demonstrates your proficiency in Angular and modern CSS. Our main focus is the frontend experience—we are looking for a "Wow" factor in the execution.

1. Technical Requirements (Stack)

> [!🤖]
> Framework: Angular 18+.
> 
> 1. CSS: Tailwind CSS.
> 
> 2. State Management: The choice of state management strategy (Signals, RxJS, etc.) is up to you. We want to see what you consider current best practice.
> 
> 3. Internationalization: Support for Portuguese (PT-PT) and English (EN).
> 
> 4. Persistence: Data must be preserved after a page refresh.

2. Mandatory Features

> [!🛠️]
> Mobile-First Approach: The application must be designed with a mobile-first mindset, ensuring an impeccable experience on small screens before scaling to Desktop.
> 
> CRUD: Full task management (create, read, update, and delete).
> 
> Filters: Filtering system by status.
> 
> Multilingual: Language toggle in the interface.
> 
> Dark Mode: Implementation of a dark theme.

3. Evaluation Criteria

> [!🎉]
> The "Wow" Factor: We want to be impressed by the polish, animations, and overall feel of the product.
> 
> Decision Making: Why did you choose a specific structure or library?
> 
> Code Quality: Strong typing (avoid any), folder organization, and clean logic.
> 
> UI/UX: Attention to visual details, hover states, and meaningful user feedback.
> 
> Git: Organization of the commit history.
